Tag 2

This is a local multiplayer game for 2 to 4 friends! One of you is the tagger and needs to chase the other players! When a player is tagged, they become the new tagger. Beware, when the timer runs out, the tagger loses!

Play tag in 9 different maps featuring a variety of jelly platforms which you can jump through, speed you up or make you jump higher! Switch things up by randomizing the platforms, granting double-jumps or allowing collisions!

Full keyboard and controller support.

Controls:

Action Keyboard Controller
Move WASD / TFGH / IJKL / arrow keys left stick, A, X
Join WASD / TFGH / IJKL / arrow keys A
Confirm enter, space start
Pause esc, P start
